Grid

The Grid System is used for layout, specifically Responsive Layouts. The Grid is made up of groupings of Rows & Columns inside 1 or more Containers.
Basicly a container contain one or more rows and a row contain between 1 and 12 column.
Let's see how it works.
For more clarity, let's assume that the container is a box with a yellow border, a row is a box fill with dark grey and a column is fill with light grey with a white border.

Just a column without parameters

Numerate Columns

A first column who take 6 column places of 12 maximum
Another column of 6 places
A first column of 3
Another column of 4
Another column of 2
Another column of 3
A row can contain until 12 column, if you set a number after "col" the column will use that number of columns in the row. <div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="col-6">A first column who take 6 column places of 12 maximum</div>
<div class="col-6">Another column of 6 places</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="col-3">A first column of 3</div>
<div class="col-4">Another column of 4</div>
<div class="col-2">Another column of 2</div>
<div class="col-3">Another column of 3</div>
</div>
</div> .container>.row>.col-6 *2 + .row>.col-3 + .col-4 + .col-2 + .col-3

Offset Columns

A column of 6 places with an offset of 6
A column of 3 with an offset of 2
Another column of 4 with an offset of 3
You can set offset to your column if you want space before it. <div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="col-6 offset-6">A column of 6 places with an offset of 6</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="col-3 offset-2">A column of 3 with an offset of 2</div>
<div class="col-4 offset-3">Another column of 4 with an offset of 3</div>
</div>
</div> .container>.row>.col-6 & .offset-6 + .row>.col-3 & .offset-2 + .col-4 & .offset-3
