Padding

The padding sits between the border and the content area. Unlike paddings you cannot have negative amounts of padding, so the value must be 0 or a positive value. Any background applied to your element will display behind the padding, and it is typically used to push the content away from the border.

For the examples below, let's assume that our HTML boxes without style are white square and the ones stylized are grey. In the stylized boxes the lighter grey is the active content box.
